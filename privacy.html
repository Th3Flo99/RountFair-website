Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

RoundFair collects no data. None.

RoundFair is built so that your data never leaves your control. There are no analytics, no trackers, no ads, no third-party SDKs, and no servers operated by RoundFair.

Where your data lives

Everything you enter in RoundFair — sessions, friends, rounds, locations, and prices — is stored on your device and in your personal iCloud (CloudKit). RoundFair has no servers and no access to any of it.

Group sharing

When you share a session with friends, the sharing happens directly between participants' iCloud accounts through Apple's CloudKit. No data passes through anyone else.

Photos

Photos for locations are added through the system photo picker. RoundFair only receives the single image you choose — never access to your photo library.

Notifications

Any notifications RoundFair shows are scheduled locally on your device. Nothing is sent from a server.

Deleting your data

Your data is yours to remove at any time:

  • Delete a session to remove it and its rounds.
  • Delete the app to remove all data from your device and your iCloud.
